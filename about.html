Welcome to Our Website and our FOUNDATION

As part of the Intelligent Science and Design Organization (ISDO), we believe that science is translated though culture and culture is translated though art. The way from science to society, thus, passes culture and art. Indeed, the three angles of Science, Culture and Art are fundamental components of society, and together they form a whole.

“Society is all and for all. Science is all and for all, when meeting culture and art”

Our Mission

We try to bring science to life by exploring it through the lens of culture and art, making it accessible and relevant to society. We integrate these three angles by publishing stories and reviving humanity. We share stories about Science with the help of Culture and Art to better express science to you and the society.

1) Stories:

Science

Exploring the world of scientific discoveries, innovations, and the lives of scientists across various fields of basic and applied sciences. Reflecting the way science connects with the world around us

Culture

Reflecting the cultural influences that shape scientific expression. Through stories from different nations and organizations, we highlight the unique ways science is interpreted and shared.

Art

Delving into how art plays a crucial role in translating scientific concepts to society, fostering innovation and enabling new perspectives on how science touches our everyday lives.

2) Reviving:

We aim to reveal the bright side of science to society and foster initiatives that make scientific progress more accessible and impactful. Our mission is to highlight how scientists and their supporters work together to create a better, more informed society.
